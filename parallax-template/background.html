Rental Insurance Policy

We do not have formal insurance on our clothes so far. However, anyone renting clothing is required to provide us with credit/debit card information. When the date of return approaches, the rentee will be notified. If the return date has passed and the item(s) have not been marked as returned by the renter, the rentee will be billed through their provided monetary information. Our formal billing policy is as follows:

  • Days 1-5 after deadline: $1 fee/day
  • -This fee will be kept by The Traveling Closet
  • Days 6 and after: $10 fee/day
  • -Untill the market value of the item(s) + 50% of the total market value has been paid by the Rentee
  • -The market value of the item(s) will be given to the Renter
  • -The 50% fee will be kept by The Traveling Closet
  • If the clothing item is returned, cleaned and without damage, 15 days or less after the deadline, the Rentee will be elligable to receive 50% of the money that has been paid by the date returned.
  • -The other 50% of money paid will be given to the Renter as compensation
  • *Note: Rentee refers to the person borrowing the clothing, and Renter is the owner of the clothing


What Are ClosetCoins(CC) ?

ClosetCoins are the currency of The Traveling Closet, which allow you to rent clothing. They were developed to encourage the availability of clothes on the website, so there would be about as many clothes available as there are checked out. Each item's value in ClosetCoins is determined by category and condition. Deterioration in the condition of an item, then, may result in a decrease in its CC value. We try to keep all item values under 20CC on average and under 30CC overall, to allow for accessibility. Every user starts with 100 closet coins. After that, renting out or donating clothes to our partners earns closet coins. The CC value earned per item is determined the same way as clothes already on the site. The CC earned will be change depending on the item's value, however, renting out items earns a bonus in CC equivalent to 5% of the items value, to encourage the availability of clothing for rental. In the future we also hope that ClosetCoins over a certain amount may be redeemed for coupons to your favourite clothing stores.
