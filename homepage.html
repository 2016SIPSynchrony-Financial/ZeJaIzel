Lend

When you make items in your closet available for borrowing, anyone can send you a request. Determine who borrows your clothing, and for how long. Every week an item is checked out, you earn 10 ClosetCoins. Save up to earn coupons to your favouirte stores!

Our Partners

We work with nonprofits like Poised for Sucess, Hope Center, Bridge to Sucess, and local shelters to give you donations to those in need. Clothes marked for Donation will be picked up and sent to our partners, who focus on giving Bisuness or formal attire to underprivelaged populations.
